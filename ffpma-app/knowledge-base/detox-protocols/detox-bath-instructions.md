# Detox Bathing Protocol

## Supplies Needed
- 2 Cups Dead Sea Salt
- 2 Cups Baking Soda
- 1/8 Cup Bentonite Clay (Borax can also be used occasionally)

## Frequency
Should be done at least 3 times per week, regardless of whether you are sick or not. Daily exposures to chemicals, EMF, and toxins require regular detox bathing in the modern world.

## How It Works
- Dead Sea Salt enters through the feet, providing much needed minerals
- Sodium Bicarbonate (Baking Soda) alkalizes the toxins being released from the skin
- Bentonite Clay binds to heavy metals and toxins, pulling them out through the skin

## Directions
1. Mix all ingredients in hot water
2. Water should be hot enough to sweat but not hot enough to burn
3. Soak for 20-30 minutes minimum
4. Meditation is advised during this time — let this be "YOU" time
5. Focus on breathing, play guided meditation, or simply relax while listening to frequency healing music
6. Take the time to detox the day away

## Quick Tips
- Drink plenty of structured water before and after bathing
- Add a pinch of Celtic salt to your water for mineral replenishment
- Best done in the evening before bed
- Dry brush skin before bathing for enhanced detoxification
- Pat dry gently after bath, do not rinse off immediately
