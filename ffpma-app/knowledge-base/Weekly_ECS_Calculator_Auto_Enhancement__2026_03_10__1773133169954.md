## Background on the Endocannabinoid System (ECS)


The ECS is a crucial component of the human body, influencing physiological processes such as mood, appetite, pain, and immune response. It consists of endogenous cannabinoids, receptors (CB1 and CB2), and enzymes responsible for synthesis and degradation. Understanding ECS tone—defined by the levels and activity of its components—is essential for developing effective therapeutic interventions.
