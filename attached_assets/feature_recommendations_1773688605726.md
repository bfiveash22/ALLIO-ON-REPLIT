## Part 2: Prioritized Feature Improvements

### 🚀 PRIORITY 1: Interactive Dosing Calculator
**Problem:** 35% of all questions relate to dosing, unit conversions, and reconstitution  
**Solution:** Build an interactive calculator with:
- mcg ↔ mg ↔ units ↔ ml conversions
- Visual syringe markings
- Reconstitution ratio calculator
- Step-by-step mixing instructions

**Expected Impact:** Reduce 25-30% of repetitive dosing questions  
**Effort Estimate:** Medium (2-3 weeks)  
**ROI:** High - addresses largest pain point

---

### 🚀 PRIORITY 2: Protocol Builder with Templates
**Problem:** Users want personalized protocols but receive generic guidance  
**Solution:** Create protocol templates for:
- Weight loss/cutting (Retatrutide, Tesamorelin/Ipamorelin)
- Muscle gain/bulking (Growth hormone peptides)
- Longevity/anti-aging (NAD+, Epitalon)
- Recovery/healing (BPC-157, TB4)
- Cognitive enhancement (Selank, Semax)

**Expected Impact:** Improved user outcomes, reduced trial-and-error  
**Effort Estimate:** High (4-6 weeks + clinical review)  
**ROI:** High - differentiates platform, improves safety

---

### 🚀 PRIORITY 3: Enhanced FAQ System
**Problem:** 43% of questions are general information requests  
**Solution:** Build comprehensive FAQ sections with:
- Top 20 questions per therapy
- Dosing FAQs with visual examples
- Safety & storage best practices
- Troubleshooting guides

**Expected Impact:** 30-40% reduction in basic questions  
**Effort Estimate:** Low-Medium (1-2 weeks)  
**ROI:** High - quick win with immediate impact

---

### 🎯 PRIORITY 4: Condition-Based Therapy Finder
**Problem:** Users can't easily find therapies for specific conditions  
**Solution:** Add search/filter by:
- Medical condition (cancer, inflammation, cognitive decline)
- Symptom (fatigue, brain fog, joint pain)
- Goal (anti-aging, performance, recovery)

**Expected Impact:** Improved therapy discovery and matching  
**Effort Estimate:** Medium (3-4 weeks)  
**ROI:** Medium-High - improves user experience

---

### 🎯 PRIORITY 5: Therapy Comparison Tool
**Problem:** Users can't compare similar therapies side-by-side  
**Solution:** Build comparison view showing:
- Benefits overlap
- Dosing differences
- Cost comparison
- Synergy potential

**Expected Impact:** Better-informed user decisions  
**Effort Estimate:** Medium (2-3 weeks)  
**ROI:** Medium - enhances decision-making

### 💡 PRIORITY 6: AI Persona Enhancements
**Problem:** AI responses may lack personalization and context  
**Solution:** Enhance AI to:
- Remember user goals across conversations
- Ask clarifying questions (age, weight, experience)
- Provide contraindication warnings

**ROI:** Medium - improves quality of interactions

---

### 💡 PRIORITY 7: Visual Administration Guides
**Problem:** Text-only instructions for complex procedures  
**Solution:** Create:
- Video tutorials for reconstitution
- Infographics for injection sites
- Syringe measurement visuals
- Storage requirement icons

**Expected Impact:** Reduced user error, increased confidence  
**Effort Estimate:** Medium (3-4 weeks for content creation)  
**ROI:** Medium - improves safety and user experience

### 💡 PRIORITY 8: Long-term Use Guidance
**Problem:** Users uncertain about long-term safety ("safe year-round?")
