# Liver & Gallbladder Cleanse Protocol

## Overview
The Liver & Gallbladder Cleanse is a structured detoxification protocol designed to support hepatobiliary function, promote bile flow, and assist the body in eliminating accumulated waste products. This protocol combines dietary modifications, targeted supplementation, and a traditional olive oil/citrus flush adapted for modern clinical practice by the Forgotten Formula PMA medical team.

## Who Is This For?
- Members with sluggish digestion or fat intolerance
- Individuals with history of gallbladder issues (non-acute)
- Those experiencing skin problems, hormonal imbalances, or chronic fatigue
- Members preparing for intensive peptide or IV therapy protocols
- Anyone seeking comprehensive liver support and detoxification

## Pre-Cleanse Assessment
Before beginning this protocol, consult your PMA physician to ensure appropriateness. The following labs are recommended:
- Comprehensive Metabolic Panel (CMP)
- Liver function tests (AST, ALT, GGT, Alkaline Phosphatase)
- Lipid panel
- Abdominal ultrasound (if gallbladder issues are suspected)

## Phase 1: Liver Preparation (Days 1-7)

### Dietary Guidelines
- **Eliminate**: Alcohol, processed foods, refined sugars, fried foods, dairy, gluten
- **Emphasize**: Organic vegetables (especially cruciferous), beets, artichokes, leafy greens
- **Include daily**: Fresh lemon water upon waking (juice of 1 lemon in 16oz warm water)
- **Healthy fats**: Small amounts of olive oil, avocado, coconut oil
- **Protein**: Moderate amounts of clean protein (wild-caught fish, pasture-raised poultry)

### Daily Supplementation
- **Milk Thistle (Silymarin)**: 600mg, divided into 2-3 doses
- **NAC (N-Acetyl Cysteine)**: 600mg twice daily
- **Dandelion Root Extract**: 500mg twice daily
- **Artichoke Leaf Extract**: 600mg daily
- **Turmeric/Curcumin**: 1000mg with black pepper extract (piperine)
- **Magnesium Citrate**: 400mg at bedtime

### Supportive Practices
- Drink minimum 80oz filtered water daily
- Organic apple juice: 8oz daily (malic acid softens gallstones)
- Castor oil packs over liver area: 30-45 minutes, 3-4 times this week
- Gentle movement: walking, yoga, stretching (avoid intense exercise)

## Phase 2: Intensification (Days 8-12)

### Modified Diet
- Continue all Phase 1 dietary guidelines
- Increase apple juice to 16oz daily
- Add beet juice: 4-8oz daily (supports bile flow)
- Reduce fat intake to minimum
- Emphasize soups, steamed vegetables, and easily digestible foods

### Enhanced Supplementation
- Continue all Phase 1 supplements
- **Add Phosphatidylcholine**: 1200mg daily (supports bile composition)
- **Add Taurine**: 1000mg daily (conjugates bile acids)
- **Digestive Bitters**: Before each meal (gentian root, wormwood blend)

### Supportive Therapies
- Castor oil packs: Daily during this phase
- **Coffee Enema** (optional): 1-2 times during this phase for enhanced liver detox
  - Use organic, mold-free coffee
  - Retain for 12-15 minutes
  - Follow with probiotic supplementation
- Infrared sauna: 20-30 minutes, every other day
- **IV Glutathione**: 1 session mid-week recommended

## Phase 3: The Flush (Day 13)

### Pre-Flush Day Schedule

**Morning through 2:00 PM**:
- Light breakfast of fruits and oatmeal
- Drink 16oz apple juice
- Continue supplements
- Eat a light, fat-free lunch by 2:00 PM

**2:00 PM - 6:00 PM**:
- No food after 2:00 PM
- Continue sipping water
- Prepare flush ingredients

### Flush Ingredients
- ½ cup extra virgin olive oil (cold-pressed, organic)
- ¾ cup fresh-squeezed grapefruit juice (or lemon/orange juice blend)
- 4 tablespoons food-grade Epsom salt (magnesium sulfate)
- 3 cups filtered water

### Evening Flush Protocol

**6:00 PM**: Mix 4 tablespoons Epsom salt in 3 cups water. Refrigerate. This makes 4 servings of ¾ cup each.

**6:00 PM**: Drink first serving of Epsom salt water (¾ cup)

**8:00 PM**: Drink second serving of Epsom salt water (¾ cup)

**9:30 PM**: Prepare olive oil mixture
- Combine ½ cup olive oil with ¾ cup fresh citrus juice
- Shake or blend vigorously until well mixed

**10:00 PM**: 
- Drink the olive oil/citrus mixture standing up
- Drink through a straw if needed for palatability
- Take 4 capsules of ornithine to promote sleep (optional)
- **Lie down immediately on your right side with knees drawn to chest**
- Remain still for at least 20 minutes (preferably fall asleep in this position)
- This position allows the mixture to reach the gallbladder

### Morning After (Day 14)

**6:00 AM or upon waking (not before 6:00 AM)**:
- Drink third serving of Epsom salt water (¾ cup)
- You may return to bed

**8:00 AM (2 hours after third serving)**:
- Drink fourth and final serving of Epsom salt water (¾ cup)

**10:00 AM (2 hours later)**:
- Begin eating with fresh fruit juice
- 30 minutes later: fresh fruit
- 1 hour later: light, regular food

### What to Expect
- Multiple bowel movements beginning the morning after the flush
- You may see green or tan-colored "stones" (cholesterol/bile aggregates)
- Some nausea is normal during the flush — it will pass
- Possible mild fatigue on the flush day and day after

## Phase 4: Post-Cleanse Recovery (Days 14-21)

### Dietary Restoration
- Gradually return to clean eating over 3-5 days
- Continue eliminating processed foods, alcohol, and excess sugar
- Emphasize anti-inflammatory foods
- Reintroduce healthy fats slowly

### Recovery Supplementation
- **Probiotics**: High-potency multi-strain, 100 billion CFU for 7 days
- **L-Glutamine**: 5g twice daily for gut lining support
- **Digestive Enzymes**: With every meal for 2 weeks
- **Continue Milk Thistle**: 600mg daily for ongoing liver support
- **Magnesium**: Continue 400mg at bedtime

### Supportive Therapies
- IV nutrient therapy within first week of recovery
- Continue gentle movement and hydration
- Resume normal exercise gradually after Day 16

## Repeat Schedule
- This protocol can be repeated every 4-6 weeks for a series of 3-6 cleanses
- Many practitioners recommend a seasonal cleanse (4x/year) for maintenance
- Allow full recovery between cleanses

## Important Safety Notes
- **Do NOT perform this cleanse if you have acute gallbladder inflammation, gallbladder infection, or bile duct obstruction**
- Consult your PMA physician before starting, especially if you have a history of gallstones
- Pregnant or nursing women should not perform this cleanse
- If you experience severe pain during the flush, discontinue and consult your physician
- This protocol is not a substitute for medical treatment of gallbladder disease
- Stay well-hydrated throughout the entire protocol
- Have someone available on flush day in case you need assistance
