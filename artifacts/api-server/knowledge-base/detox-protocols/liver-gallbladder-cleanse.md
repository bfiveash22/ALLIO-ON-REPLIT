# Liver/Gallbladder Cleanse Protocol

## Overview
The Liver/Gallbladder Cleanse is a critical detoxification protocol designed to flush accumulated gallstones, bile sludge, and toxins from the liver and gallbladder. This cleanse is especially important for patients who still have their gallbladder, and can also benefit those who have had gallbladder removal (cholecystectomy) by supporting liver bile duct drainage.

## Preparation Phase (6 Days Before Cleanse)
1. Drink 1 liter of apple juice daily for 6 days (malic acid softens gallstones)
2. Alternatively: Take malic acid supplements (1500-2000mg daily)
3. Eat light, low-fat meals during preparation
4. Avoid cold foods and beverages
5. Stay well hydrated with structured water

## Cleanse Day Protocol
### Afternoon
- Eat a light lunch before 2:00 PM (no fat, no protein)
- Do NOT eat or drink after 2:00 PM (except water)

### 6:00 PM - First Epsom Salt Drink
- Mix 1 tablespoon Epsom salt (magnesium sulfate) in 3/4 cup warm water
- Drink the mixture (it opens bile ducts)

### 8:00 PM - Second Epsom Salt Drink
- Repeat same Epsom salt mixture

### 9:45 PM - Prepare the Flush Mixture
- 1/2 cup cold-pressed extra virgin olive oil
- 2/3 cup fresh grapefruit juice (or orange + lemon juice)
- Mix well in a jar, shake vigorously

### 10:00 PM - Drink the Flush Mixture
- Drink the entire olive oil/citrus mixture standing up
- Immediately lie down on your RIGHT side with knees pulled up toward chest
- Stay in this position for at least 20 minutes (do not move)
- Try to sleep in this position

## Morning After
### 6:00 AM - Third Epsom Salt Drink
- Repeat Epsom salt mixture upon waking

### 8:00 AM - Fourth Epsom Salt Drink
- Final Epsom salt dose

### 10:00 AM - Resume Eating
- Start with fresh fruit juice
- Gradually return to normal diet (light foods first)

## Expected Results
- Green, tan, or brown stones may be visible in bowel movements
- Stones range from pea-sized to golf ball-sized
- Chaff-like particles (cholesterol crystals) are common
- Multiple cleanses may be needed (every 2-4 weeks) until no more stones appear
- Typically 6-12 cleanses needed for full clearing

## Important Notes
- If gallbladder has been removed: Protocol still benefits liver bile duct drainage
- Timing is important: Best done on a weekend when you can rest
- Some nausea is normal — the olive oil stimulates massive bile release
- Hydrate well with structured water throughout
- Consider doing a parasite cleanse first (parasites can block bile ducts)
- Follow with coffee enemas for additional liver support

## Contraindications
- Active gallbladder attack or inflammation
- Severe liver disease without medical supervision
- Pregnancy
- Children under 16 without medical guidance
