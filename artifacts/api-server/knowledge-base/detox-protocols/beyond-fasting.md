# Beyond Fasting: Metabolic Reset & Cellular Detox

## Overview
The Beyond Fasting protocol is a 7-week metabolic reset designed to transition the body from being a "sugar burner" to an efficient "fat burner" (fat adaptation). This preparation maximizes the healing benefits of the subsequent 5-day water fast by stimulating autophagy and stem cell production from Day 1.

## Who Is This For?
- Members struggling with weight loss resistance
- Individuals with brain fog, low energy, or chronic fatigue
- Those seeking to optimize longevity and cellular health
- Members preparing for intensive peptide or regenerative therapies
- Anyone looking to reset their metabolism and immune system

## The 7-Week Journey

### Week 1: Ketogenic Transition
- **Goal**: Start fat adaptation by shifting to a high-fat, moderate-protein, low-carb diet.
- **Action**: Keep net carbohydrates under 50g per day.
- **Focus**: Healthy fats (avocado, olive oil, grass-fed butter) and fiber-rich vegetables.

### Week 2: Eating Less Often
- **Goal**: Introduce intermittent fasting.
- **Action**: Implement a 16:8 eating window (16 hours fasting, 8 hours eating).
- **Focus**: Eliminate snacking between meals to stabilize insulin.

### Week 3: The Eating Window
- **Goal**: Narrow the eating window to further fat adaptation.
- **Action**: Move toward an 18:6 or 20:4 window as comfortably tolerable.
- **Focus**: Maintain high-quality nutrient density during the window.

### Week 4: Diet Variation (5-1-1 Rule)
- **Goal**: Metabolic flexibility through "Feast/Famine" cycling.
- **Action**: 5 days Keto/Intermittent Fasting, 1 day 24-hour fast, 1 day "Feast" (higher healthy carbs).
- **Focus**: Hormonal optimization and preventing metabolic adaptation.

### Week 5: Advanced Variation (4-2-1 Rule)
- **Goal**: Deepen cellular preparation for the extended fast.
- **Action**: 4 days Keto, 2 days 24-hour fasts, 1 day "Feast".
- **Focus**: Cellular cleanup (autophagy) begins to increase.

### Week 6: The 5-Day Water Fast
- **Goal**: Maximum autophagy and stem cell mobilization.
- **Action**: Water only (or Fasting Mimicking Diet) for 5 consecutive days.
- **Focus**: Rest, meditation, and allowing the body's innate intelligence to heal.

### Week 7: The Refeed
- **Goal**: Proper transition back to food to lock in results.
- **Action**: Break the fast slowly with bone broth, then fermented foods, then light meals.
- **Focus**: Rebuilding the gut microbiome and supporting new stem cell integration.

| Blood Ketones | 0.5 - 3.0 mmol/L | Nutritional ketosis/fat burning |
| GKI Index | < 3.0 | Therapeutic autophagy zone |
| Heart Rate Variability | Upward trend | Parasympathetic nervous system recovery |

## Required Support Protocols
- **Hydration**: Minimum 80oz filtered water with electrolytes (sea salt/magnesium).
- **Sleep**: 7-9 hours of quality sleep to support growth hormone release.
- **Stress**: Gentle movement (walking/yoga) only; avoid high-intensity exercise during Week 6.
- **Detox**: Daily dry brushing and 2-3 detox baths per week (see Detox Bath Instructions).

## Important Safety Notes
- Consult your PMA physician before beginning, especially if you have metabolic conditions or are on medication.
- If you feel lightheaded or weak during the fast, supplement with electrolytes immediately.
- Do not engage in heavy machinery operation or intense travel during the Week 6 fast.
- This protocol is not recommended for those with active eating disorders, pregnancy, or Type 1 Diabetes without direct medical supervision.
