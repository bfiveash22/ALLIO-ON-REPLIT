## 2. Methodology: Clinical Trial Data Query & Analysis


- **Data Sources:**  
  Recent and ongoing clinical trials were reviewed from major repositories including ClinicalTrials.gov, PubMed, and select PMA-shared proprietary databases. Emphasis was on studies published and updated within the last 12 months.


- **Inclusion Criteria:**  
  - Human trials assessing ECS tone variations  
  - Studies measuring endogenous cannabinoid levels (AEA, 2-AG) and receptor densities (CB1, CB2)  
  - Investigations linking ECS deficiencies to inflammatory, neurological, and metabolic conditions  
  - Trials evaluating peptide or natural compound modulation of ECS  


- **Analysis Approach:**  
  Findings were synthesized on ECS variable relevance, weighted by sample size, methodological quality, clinical relevance, and biological significance. Special attention was given to non-synthetic, nature-derived ECS modulators.
