# Parasite Cleansing Protocol

## Overview

Parasitic infections are more common than generally recognized, even in developed countries. They can contribute to chronic fatigue, digestive issues, skin conditions, nutrient deficiencies, and immune dysregulation. This protocol provides a structured approach to parasite identification, elimination, and prevention using time-tested herbal formulas alongside modern nutritional science.

## Signs & Symptoms of Parasitic Infection

### Digestive Symptoms
- Chronic bloating, gas, and abdominal discomfort
- Alternating constipation and diarrhea
- Unexplained nausea
- Mucus in stool
- Anal itching (especially at night)
- Increased appetite or unexplained weight loss

### Systemic Symptoms
- Chronic fatigue and malaise
- Joint and muscle pain
- Skin rashes, eczema, or hives
- Teeth grinding (bruxism), especially during sleep
- Iron-deficiency anemia despite adequate intake
- Brain fog and cognitive difficulties
- Mood disturbances: anxiety, depression, irritability
- Insomnia or restless sleep

## Pre-Cleanse Preparation (Weeks 1–2)

### Drainage Pathway Support
Before initiating parasite elimination, ensure drainage pathways are open:

- **Bowel regularity**: Minimum 1–2 bowel movements daily
  - Magnesium citrate: 400–800 mg at bedtime
  - Adequate hydration: half body weight in ounces of filtered water
  - Fiber: 25–35 g daily from whole food sources
- **Liver support**: Milk thistle (silymarin) 300 mg 2x daily
- **Lymphatic support**: Dry brushing, rebounding, or lymphatic massage
- **Kidney support**: Adequate hydration, dandelion root tea

### Dietary Preparation
- Eliminate sugar, refined carbohydrates, and processed foods
- Reduce fruit intake to low-sugar options (berries, green apples)
- Increase anti-parasitic foods: raw garlic, pumpkin seeds, papaya seeds, coconut oil, pineapple
- Add fermented foods for immune support

## Phase 1: Herbal Antiparasitic Protocol (Weeks 3–8)

### Core Herbal Formula (The "Big Three")

**Black Walnut Hull Tincture (Green)**
- Week 3: Start with 5 drops in water, 2x daily
- Week 4: Increase to 10 drops, 2x daily
- Week 5–8: 15–20 drops, 2x daily
- Take on empty stomach, 30 minutes before meals

**Wormwood (Artemisia absinthium)**
- Week 3: 200 mg daily
- Week 4: 400 mg daily
- Week 5–8: 600 mg daily (divided into 2 doses)
- Take with meals

**Clove (Syzygium aromaticum)**
- Week 3: 500 mg daily
- Week 4: 500 mg 2x daily
- Week 5–8: 500 mg 3x daily
- Take with meals; addresses parasite eggs

### Additional Antiparasitic Support
- Mimosa pudica seed: 2 capsules 2x daily on empty stomach (biofilm disruption)
- Diatomaceous earth (food grade): 1 tsp in water, increasing to 1 tbsp daily
- Berberine: 500 mg 2x daily
- Oregano oil: 150 mg emulsified, 2x daily

### Biofilm Disruption Protocol
Parasites often hide within biofilm communities:
- NAC (N-Acetyl Cysteine): 600 mg 2x daily
- Serrapeptase: 120,000 SPU on empty stomach
- Humic/fulvic acid: as directed
- InterFase Plus or equivalent enzyme formula

## Phase 2: Full Moon Intensification

Parasites are more active during full moon cycles due to melatonin and serotonin fluctuations:

- Increase herbal dosing by 25–50% for 5 days surrounding each full moon
- Add binding agents during intensification:
  - Activated charcoal: 500 mg 2x daily (away from food/supplements by 2 hours)
  - Bentonite clay: 1 tsp in water, 1x daily
  - Modified citrus pectin: as directed
- Track symptom patterns relative to lunar cycles

## Phase 3: Maintenance & Prevention (Ongoing)

### Monthly Maintenance Pulse
- 5-day herbal antiparasitic pulse each month (around full moon)
- Continue pumpkin seed consumption: 1/4 cup daily
- Garlic: 2 raw cloves daily or aged garlic extract

### Dietary Anti-Parasitic Foods
- Raw garlic (allicin is a potent antiparasitic)
- Pumpkin seeds (cucurbitins paralyze worms)
- Papaya seeds (blended into smoothies)
- Coconut oil (medium-chain fatty acids are antiparasitic)
- Fermented foods for immune resilience
- Apple cider vinegar: 1 tbsp in water before meals

### Hygiene & Prevention
- Wash hands thoroughly before meals and after restroom use
- Wash all produce in vinegar-water solution
- Cook meats to proper internal temperatures
- Filter drinking water
- Deworm household pets regularly
- Avoid walking barefoot in areas with animal waste
- Change bedding weekly during active cleansing

## Managing Herxheimer (Die-Off) Reactions

### Common Die-Off Symptoms
- Headaches, fatigue, body aches
- Skin breakouts or rashes
- Increased digestive discomfort
- Mood changes, irritability
- Flu-like symptoms

### Die-Off Management Strategies
- Reduce antiparasitic dosing temporarily
- Increase binder intake (charcoal, clay, chlorella)
- Epsom salt baths: 2 cups in warm bath, 20 minutes
- Coffee enemas for liver support (see Detox Protocol)
- Infrared sauna: 20–30 minutes
- Rest and adequate sleep
- Increase hydration

## Special Populations

### Children (Under Practitioner Supervision)
- Use age-appropriate herbal formulations
- Lower dosing based on body weight
- Pumpkin seed butter as food-based approach
- Papaya seed smoothies
- Focus on hygiene education

### Pregnant/Nursing Women
- Avoid wormwood and black walnut hull
- Safe options: pumpkin seeds, garlic, coconut oil
- Consult with practitioner before any supplementation

## Testing & Monitoring

### Recommended Testing
- Comprehensive stool analysis with O&P (ova and parasites)
- GI-MAP PCR testing for specific organisms
- Blood work: CBC with differential, eosinophil count
- Consider specialty labs: Doctor's Data, Genova, DiagnosTechs

### Progress Markers
- Symptom improvement tracking
- Stool changes (may see expelled organisms)
- Energy level improvements
- Sleep quality improvement
- Skin clearing
- Repeat testing at 3 and 6 months

## Contraindications & Warnings
- This protocol is educational and should be implemented under qualified practitioner supervision
- Wormwood is contraindicated in pregnancy and nursing
- Individuals on blood thinners should avoid high-dose garlic and clove
- Severe infestations may require pharmaceutical intervention
- Monitor liver function during extended herbal protocols
