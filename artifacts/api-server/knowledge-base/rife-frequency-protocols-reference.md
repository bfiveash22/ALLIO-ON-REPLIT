## Universal Safety Guidelines

### General Safety
- Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before starting any Rife frequency protocol
- Rife therapy is complementary and should not replace conventional medical treatment
- Start all protocols with the lowest intensity and shortest duration
- Maintain adequate hydration (2-3 liters daily) during treatment
- Allow 24-48 hours between sessions for detoxification

### Universal Contraindications
- Pregnancy and breastfeeding
- Cardiac pacemakers, defibrillators, or implanted electronic medical devices
- Epilepsy or seizure disorders
- Active organ transplant immunosuppression
- Children under 5 without specialized practitioner guidance

### Herxheimer Reaction Management
If experiencing die-off symptoms (fatigue, headache, joint pain, brain fog):
1. Reduce session frequency and duration
2. Increase water intake to 3-4 liters daily
3. Take binders (activated charcoal, bentonite clay) 30 min after sessions
4. Epsom salt baths and infrared sauna support elimination
5. Rest and resume at lower intensity

### Equipment Types
- **Plasma Devices:** Emit frequencies through noble gas plasma. Maintain 12-18 inches distance. Best for whole-body and deep-tissue applications.
- **Contact Devices:** Deliver frequencies through direct skin contact via pads/electrodes. Best for targeted local application.
- **PEMF Devices:** Full-body mats delivering low-frequency therapeutic fields. Best for systemic inflammation and pain protocols.
